  \hline
  \textbf{Term} & \textbf{Definition} \\ \hline
  Proposer    & A first-tier LLM in the MoA pipeline that produces a candidate summary draft \\ \hline
  Aggregator  & The second-tier LLM in the MoA pipeline that fuses the proposer drafts into a final answer \\ \hline
  Residual connection (MoA) & Injection of the original source article into the aggregator prompt so that fusion stays grounded \\ \hline
  Rubric judging   & LLM-as-judge that scores a summary on multiple named criteria with a numerical scale \\ \hline
  Pairwise judging & LLM-as-judge that compares two summaries A vs.\ B and selects a winner \\ \hline
  Fleiss' $\kappa$ & Inter-rater agreement statistic for categorical ratings by $\geq 2$ raters \\ \hline
  Sign test        & Non-parametric test for paired binary outcomes, used here for pairwise win rates \\ \hline
  Length bucketing & Stratification of pairwise verdicts by output-length ratio to control length bias \\ \hline
  Entailment       & Logical relation: hypothesis is supported by premise; used for claim-level factuality \\ \hline
